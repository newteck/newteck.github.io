Company

LLC Newteck Well Service is 100% Russian company having at its disposal advanced domestic and western technologies for stimulation of oil and gas wells of any complexity.
Newteck Well Service has all required material and technical facilities to perform productive operations, meeting the requirements of oil and gas production companies.
Directed by a competent management, whom many oil and gas companies know firsthand, highly qualified engineers and technicians with long-term employment history work in the Company.

Company’s business activity

  • Proppant-based fracs
  • Acid fracs, bottomhole treatment
  • CBM fracs
  • Multi-stage fracs
  • Fracs in shale beds with high flow rate
  • Engineering support and supply of bottomhole assemblies for MS fracs
