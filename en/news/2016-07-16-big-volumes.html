Year 2016: High volume fracs

16 JUL 2016

High volume fracs including multi-stage fracs for "RN-Yuganskneftegaz" (JSC "NK" Rosneft ")

Since January 2016, the we carries out large-fracturing jot at Prirazlomnoye oilfield (Pravdinskaya area) with the injection of up to 250 tons of proppant per operation.

From February to March 2016, thanks to the coordinated work of production and technological departments with the involvement of additional specialized fracturing technology and other equipment, the Company has been able to exceed the planed production by 21%.

As a result, for the 1st quarter of 2016, our company "Newteck Well Service" LLC. entered the top three rated fracturing service companies in the "RN-Yuganskneftegaz" rating.

Customer notes the high level of technological readiness of "Newteck Well Service" LLC. to perform the given tasks, but also to achieve a high percentage of the estimated flow rate of the liquid. Thanks to our professional work actual fluid flow rate after completing was 62% higher than expected based on the calculation.
