Multistage fracturing on J-0 deposits

14 JAN 2016

Newteck Well Service continues to conquer J0. We are pleased to share with our current and potential customers of their experience and best practices.

At the end of 2015 Newteck has brilliantly carried out a series of hi-rate multistage operations on Palyanovskoye field.

There were 9 stages, Slickwater technology was applied.

The Client was LLC Gazpromneft-Khantos.

Frac jobs were performed in 1000 m horizontal well on J0 deposits the rate of 14 m3 / min.

High pumping rate was easily attained due to combined frac fleet consisting of 9 pumps, so the total capacity reached 22 500 hp.

In addition 2000 m³ and 600 m³ frac tank were used, as well as 4 HP lines.
