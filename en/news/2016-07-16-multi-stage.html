Multi-stage frac: 30

16 JUL 2016

In June 2016 "Newteck Well Service" LLC. has successfully completed the 30 stage job for "Gazpromneft-Khantos" LLC., a subsidiary of Gazprom Neft.

Multi-stage aproach not only significantly reduces time needed to complete the well, but also allows you to get results in hard-to-recover oil fields, it also increases the number of zones completed in one well.

The 30-stage frac job was executed using completion tool where sliding frac ports were activated using coil tubing (CT) - a good alternative to ball drop systems that are widely known in Russia . This technology allows you to engage in the development of new areas of difficult reserves, which can not be used with the currently existing technologies. Exclusivity in its speed, which significantly reduces the time between cycles.

The work was performed on a 178 mm (7") casing with 114 mm cemented liner. The wellhead was equipped with a device to provide safety and protect the CT. Actuated frac sleeve were opened by usin CT shifting tool, if necessary, they can be closed with the same tool. This method allows for the activation of the sleeve every 40 minutes. This technology delivered colossal benefits - save time and resources at the same time. This systm eliminates the technical limitations on the number of processing steps. Besides the saved time & resources during the complettion of the well, "GPN-Khantos" LLLC. expecting to get a significant economi impact.

Thanks to the coordinated work of the engineering and production stuff and technological services using a specialized equipment and technology, non-standard approach, the company was able to minimize the prep time per each stage of the hydraulic fracturing. "Newteck Well Service" LLC. set a record among the Russian well service companies not only on the total number of stages per well (historical maximum - 21 stages!), But also fracturing the maximum number of stages per day: 7 stages in 21 hours!!! To date, the average number of stages per 24 hours did not exceed 3-4.

Work on this well become part of "Lean Production" initiative, started in "Gazpromneft - Khantos". The effectiveness of the technology and the implementation of a record number of frac will be the basis for further optimization of the production of oil and gas companies.

After the analysis of economic efficiency was completed, this method completing the wells is planed to be "replicate" on all the similar wells of "Gazprom Neft".
